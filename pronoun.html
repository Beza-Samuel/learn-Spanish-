Pronouns in Spanish

Yo - I

Tú - You (informal singular)

Usted - You (formal singular)

Él - He

Ella - She

Nosotros - We (masculine or mixed group)

Nosotras - We (feminine)

Vosotros - You all (informal, masculine or mixed, used in Spain)

Vosotras - You all (informal feminine, used in Spain)

Ustedes - You all (formal plural in Spain, plural in Latin America)

Ellos - They (masculine or mixed group)

Ellas - They (feminine)
