Basic Verbs in Spanish

Ser - To Be

Yo soy - I am

Tú eres - You are (informal)

Él / Ella es - He / She is

Usted es - You are (formal)

Nosotros / Nosotras somos - We are

Vosotros / Vosotras sois - You all are (informal, Spain)

Ellos / Ellas son - They are

Ustedes son - You all are (formal in Spain, plural in Latin America)

Tener - To Have

Yo tengo - I have

Tú tienes - You have (informal)

Él / Ella tiene - He / She has

Usted tiene - You have (formal)

Nosotros / Nosotras tenemos - We have

Vosotros / Vosotras tenéis - You all have (informal, Spain)

Ellos / Ellas tienen - They have

Ustedes tienen - You all have (formal in Spain, plural in Latin America)
