Articles in Spanish

Definite Articles (The)

El - The (masculine singular)

La - The (feminine singular)

Los - The (masculine plural)

Las - The (feminine plural)

Indefinite Articles (A / An / Some)

Un - A / An (masculine singular)

Una - A / An (feminine singular)

Unos - Some (masculine plural)

Unas - Some (feminine plural)

Tip: Definite articles are used when talking about a **specific thing**. Indefinite articles are used when talking about **any one of something**.
